HTML Tags

<!doctype HTML>
The first thing we add to our HTML document which defines what is the type of this document

<html></html>
The next and most important tag on our page

<title></title>
The title of the webpage which will be displayed on top of the tab in a browser

<nav></nav>
The navigation of the page, this will contain the links to other pages of the website

<article></article>
Tag used for containing independent piece of content. It could be shared as stand alone piece of content and will still make sense
